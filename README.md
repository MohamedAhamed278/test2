# Transition Requirements

This document outlines the **Transition Requirements** for launching and scaling a food delivery platform connecting home cooks with customers.

---

## 1. Technical Transition Requirements

* Migrating existing data to the new platform.
* Integrating secure payment gateways.
* Configuring the application for **iOS**, **Android**, and backend servers.
* Ensuring reliable API connectivity with third-party delivery services.

---

## 2. Operational Transition Requirements

* Training home cooks on how to use the platform:

  * Listing meals
  * Managing orders
  * Updating availability
* Training cooks on how to evaluate and price products.
* Training customer support and operations teams on the new system.
* Developing **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)** for:

  * Order handling
  * Quality control
  * Dispute resolution

---

## 3. Legal & Compliance Requirements

* Obtaining the necessary business licenses to operate a food delivery platform.
* Drafting clear contracts for:

  * Home cooks
  * Delivery riders
* Ensuring compliance with local food safety and hygiene regulations.

---

## 4. Marketing & Onboarding Requirements

* Launching an awareness campaign to attract both cooks and customers.
* Creating onboarding materials for cooks, such as:

  * Instructional videos
  * User guides
* Educating customers on how to use the platform and highlighting its benefits compared to traditional methods.

---

## 5. Phasing & Pilot Requirements

* Starting operations in a limited geographic area before scaling.
* Running a beta test with a small group of trusted cooks and early adopters.
* Collecting feedback and implementing improvements before the full launch.
